Advantages of working/applying with us

Here is a guide on how you can best work with us during your job hunt.

1

Apply for a job

When you apply online, you’re doubling your efforts. You’ll create a Impacto account where you can check the status of your application. Plus, the Talent Agent filling the position will be alerted that you’re interested. If they think you’re a good fit, they’ll contact you directly.

2

Stay Current

Our Talent Agents will only reach out when they find the right person for the right job. That’s why we encourage you to keep in touch with us and keep applying for any positions you like. We also recommend you to log into Impacto frequently to check the status of your job applications, keep your contact info current and — if you’re a creative — your portfolio up-to-date.

3

Let’s Chat

We don’t like to do business with strangers, so please contact our office to speak to a Talent Agent so that we can get to know you. Your happiness and success is as vital to us as our clients, which is why we want to find you work that’s more than simply a good fit for your skills, but somewhere you can thrive.
